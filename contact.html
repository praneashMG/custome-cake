Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries about our catering and custom orders.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance for custom cake pops and dessert tables. For large weddings and corporate events (100+ guests), please book 6-8 weeks ahead.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes! Tastings are available Monday-Thursday by appointment. A small fee applies, which is credited toward your final order.

What's your delivery radius?

We deliver within 50 miles of Los Angeles. Delivery is free within 30 miles. Travel fees apply for locations beyond.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, and vegan options for many of our desserts. Please specify when ordering.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refund available up to 14 days before event. 50% refund between 7-13 days. No refunds within 7 days of event.

Do you provide setup and cleanup?

Yes! All catering packages include professional delivery, setup, and styling. Post-event pickup is available for Platinum packages.
